Part 2 One way creativity addresses basic human needs, meaning, or personal fulfillment

In my daily life, I exercise my creativity mainly in four different kinds of activities- software development, cooking, painting, and poem writing.

 

Software Development: As a software engineer working in the Silicon Valley for more than 30 years, I work on creativity daily in my job. Software development is often a balance between feature, cost, time, memory usage, and quality. Therefore, software engineers need to craft creative solutions by using computer languages to express their creative solution. Oftentimes, the most creative solution uses very short expression and less memory to achieve high quality very quickly and non creative solutions waste cost and time. While people often consider Software Development to be an engineering work, the most clever solutions are often highly artistic. The creativity in Software Development requires carefully understanding the problem and exploring various possible solutions, and most importantly, recognize the changing context of our environment. Very frequently, a great solution comes out at the right time, and not achievable before its time, simply because the surrounding supporting economic environment is not mature, or cost effective, for that before. 

 

Cooking: In my personal life, I enjoy creativity in my daily cooking. I combine different kinds of hot pepper to create a very rich level of hot dishes to serve my friends. Cooking good food often requires me to put different fresh ingredients together and explore new techniques. It not only fulfills the very basic human need of hunger and seeking for satisfaction, but also very meaningful as a way to show my love to the one I serve my food. Nothing is more satisfying after seeing my fellow students finish my food and pack all the leftovers home after dinner.

 

Portrait Painting: I painted many portraits of my friends to show my love to them. I also paint many self portraits. While my friends and family saw my finished work, they knew how much I loved them. They know I am willing to spend time rendering their faces and praying for them during the work. It became a way for me to express my affection to my friends. Painting self portraits helps me to reexamine my own identity and inner feelings. Seeing my completed works and exhibiting them to others also fulfills my big ego. 

 

Poem Writing: Poem writing helps me to crystallize my memory, good or bad, big or small, joy or sorrow, of a particular moment. I wrote several hundred Chinese poems and archived tem in https://github.com/FrankYFTang/FrankYungFongTangArt/tree/main/ChinesePoemsLinks to an external site. . I also wrote and blogged some English poems in https://github.com/FrankYFTang/FrankYungFongTangArt/blob/main/EnglishReflectionPoems.mdLinks to an external site. . I am very honor that De Anza English department publish one of them, ‚ÄúA Time of Destruction‚Äù, in Red Wheelbarrow Student Edition, 2025 ( https://www.deanza.edu/english/creative-writing/documents/2025-Red-Wheelbarrow-Student-Edition.pdf#page=96Links to an external site. ) It is a poem I wrote to memorize the destruction of Flint Center at De Anza college. Software development, cooking, painting, and poem writing are all dealing with creativity. These activities explore different kinds of intelligence and I enjoy them all.
